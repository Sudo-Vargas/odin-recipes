Banana Bread

Description

Fresh baked banana bread

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • White sugar
  • Chopped pecans
  • Eggs
  • Very ripe bananas
  • Vegetable oil
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Grease and flour two 8"x4" loaf pans. Set aside
  3. Sift flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar
  4. Stir in nuts, eggs, bananas, oil, and vanilla extract
  5. Pour batter into pans.
  6. Bake for 45-60 minutes. Cool in pans on wire rack for 10 min before serving.
