Oatmeal Cookies

Description

Some delicous oatmeal and raisin cookies

Ingredients

  • Softened butter
  • White sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Egg
  • Vanilla extract
  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Oats
  • Raisins
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Whisk butter and sugars in a bowl
  3. Add egg and vanilla
  4. Add dry ingredients
  5. Stir together
  6. Fold in oats and raisins
  7. Roll into 1 1/2" balls and put on cookie sheet
  8. Bake for 13 minutes
